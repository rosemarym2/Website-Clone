Out and about


Best walks for autumn highlights＞

Now's the perfect time for a walk as the great outdoors is transformed by the glorious golds, reds and greens of autumn. Here are some of the best walks to help you explore the parks, coast and countryside we look after at this magical time of year.


October half term events＞

Get outside with the family and create unforgettable autumn moments this half term, or step inside the houses in our care to discover lots more to do. From woodland adventure days to foodie fun, it’ll be impossible for the kids to get bored over the school holidays.


Autumn activities for all the family＞

The vibrant colours, bold trees and rich harvests of autumn are here. Whether you're after family fun or some quiet time alone, explore the best experiences this season has to offer wherever you are with our range of autumnal activities.


Spooky Halloween adventures＞

Join us for some Halloween family fun as ghosts and ghouls come out to play, pumpkins do their worst and the places we look after reveal their mysterious side. Check out the types of events happening at many of the places in our care and plan a spooky day out.


Cauldrons to coffins: creepy curios in our collection＞

To help you get into the Halloween spirit, we've put together a selection of macabre and creepy objects from our collections that will send shivers down your spine. Discover the stories behind them, if you dare...

Join us at the AGM＞

We're getting ready for the next members' Annual General Meeting. It's being held at 10am on Saturday 30 October 2021 at the Harrogate Convention Centre, North Yorkshire. Online voting has now closed, but you can register to attend the AGM on the day and vote for the resolutions then. It's your chance, as members, to help us make the best possible decisions for the Trust in the months to come.

Have your say

Getting ready for COP26 

With nations around the world reporting on plans to significantly cut carbon emissions, this year is a turning point for action on climate change. Find out what we’ll be discussing at the Climate Resilience Heritage Summit on 27 October and more about our presence at COP26, which opens in Glasgow on 31 October. Plus, get some ideas on what you can do to join in the fight against the climate crisis.
